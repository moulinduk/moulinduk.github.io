About me

I recently completed my Integrated Master of Science (BSc+MSc) in Physics from IIT Kharagpur, graduating with a CGPA of 8.85. I am excited to continue my academic journey as a PhD student specializing in theoretical high energy physics. Throughout my studies, I have gained valuable experience through various summer research internships, including an international internship at McMaster University in Canada through the MITACS 2023 program.

In addition to my academic pursuits, I have a strong passion for web design. I served as the lead backend developer for Spring Fest 2022, the annual cultural fest at IIT Kharagpur, where I honed my skills in creating dynamic and user-friendly web applications.

Beyond these, I am a dedicated practitioner of Bhakti Yoga, an ancient meditation practice from India that I find deeply enriching.

Traveling is another passion of mine. I love exploring new places and whenever I travel,make it a point to visit renowned spots, especially those known for their natural beauty.
